About Us

Meet the Teacher

The PS Lab is ran by Mr. Roeth, an avid programmer who has been in the industry for 30 years. He has experience in many programming languages such as Java (his favorite), C#. C++, COBOL, python, Javascript, and plenty more.
Mr. Roeth is a dedicated teacher who spends the majority of his time preparing students to take their next step.

Our Lab

The Programming and Software Lab (PS Lab) is built to mimic an office environment. It allows you to have your own area to work, without prohibitting a collaborative environment.

Community

The PS Lab is all about connectivity and learning together. Fellow peers are used often as a way to help eachother find out new things beyond what the lab teaches. If you want to explore something, you almost always can.
Projects in lab often have the option to be a partnered project, and whether you choose to or not, you will often find yourself collaborating with peers to find out how things work.
